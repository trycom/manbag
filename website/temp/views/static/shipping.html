7. I still haven't received my package 7 days after it was shipped. What do I do?

If a package has not arrived after 7 days from the day you received your tracking number in your shipment confirmation email, we assume it has been lost. If this is the case, please contact us at help@getbrandid.com with your Order # from your original order confirmation email. Depending on availability of stock, we will either send a replacement or refund your purchase assuming the package has been lost.

  • If the packaging has been lost by the courier, depending on availability of stock, we will either send a replacement or refund your purchase.
  • If the item turns up after you have accepted a refund and you want to return the item, we will contact you to arrange a free collection. You will be legally liable to take reasonable care of the item during this time.
  • If the item turns up after you have accepted a refund and you want to keep the item we email you a link where we will require you to make a payment for the item.
  • If the items turns up late, but you haven’t processed a refund, write to us at help@getbrandid.com call us on +44 (0) 203 322 2882 and we will refund you the delivery charge.
  • We consider packages delivered when they are signed for, regardless of who accepts the package. For example, if the package is delivered to your house but signed for by your neighbour, we do not accept any responsibility for the item reaching you after it has been signed for.

8. Can I combine multiple orders and pay only once for shipping?

Yes, provided the orders all have the same delivery address, but you must contact us at help@getbrandid.com or call us on +44 (0) 203 322 2882 with an Order #, and we will make sure all packages you purchased on that day are sent in one shipment. We will refund your second shipping charge as soon as we receive your email.

9. What happens if the courier doesn't find me at home?

You will receive a note in your postbox with instructions of how to either arrange a free redelivery or collect the package from your local depot.

If you have any questions about your delivery, contact us at help@getbrandid.com or call us on +44 (0) 203 322 2882 to arrange another delivery. At this point you will be liable for any redelivery charges.

NB: if you have provided an incorrect delivery address, you will be charged for redelivery.
